Our services

PPD Bespoke LTD Painter and Decorator

Our service company specializes in top-notch painting and decoration solutions. We transform spaces with skilled painting techniques and creative decor, exceeding expectations.

Painter

Painters apply paint to surfaces, adding color, protection, and enhancing aesthetics to create beautiful and refreshed spaces.

Decorator

Decorators transform spaces with creative flair, selecting furnishings, accessories, and colors to achieve a harmonious and stylish ambiance.

Designer

A room designer creates aesthetically pleasing and functional spaces through layout, color, furniture, and decor selections.

Preparing

Preparing walls and wood for painting involves cleaning, sanding, and priming surfaces to ensure a smooth and durable finish.
